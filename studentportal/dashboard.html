About CHARUSAT

Established in 2009, CHARUSAT is a prominent university offering a wide range of programs in engineering, pharmacy, management, and more. It aims to nurture socially responsible and globally competent professionals.

  • NAAC A+ Accredited University
  • Top 200 Universities by NIRF
  • Top 3 Universities in Gujarat by GSIRF
  • Member of United Nations Academic Impact
  • Ranked in Q2 Innovation (SCIMAGO Rankings)
  • Recognized as Center of Excellence by Government of Gujarat
