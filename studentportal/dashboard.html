About CHARUSAT

Established in 2009, CHARUSAT is a prominent university offering a wide range of programs in engineering, pharmacy, management, and more.
It aims to nurture socially responsible and globally competent professionals.

  • NAAC A+ Accredited University
  • Ranked among Top 200 Universities by NIRF
  • Ranked among Top 3 Universities in Gujarat by GSIRF
  • Member of United Nations of Academic Impact
  • Achieved Q2 Innovation Rank in 110th IND SCIMAGO Institutions Rankings
  • Certified as Center of Excellence by Government of Gujarat
